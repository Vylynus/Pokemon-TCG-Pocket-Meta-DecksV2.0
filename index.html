Tier

The top tiers of this Tier List (S rank) are Decks that can function with minimal ease for best results. Gyarados ex, Pikachu ex, and Mewtwo ex decks are all quick to set up, relatively powerful and are consistent, which is key to a deck's success.

A+ Tier Decks are also at the top of the metagame but may have to work a little harder to compete against the S Tiers.

A Tier Decks are also powerful and can also function at a high level, but may not be as easy to pilot as the S and A+ Tiers.

B Tier Decks are decent, but would require more skill to achieve success with.

C Tier consists of unexplored Rogue decks, which all have potential but would require a skilled pilot to find success with.

D Tier consists of F2P Friendly decks that are good on their own. These decks have specific niches in the metagame and are outclassed by decks in the higher tiers.

These decks have been arranged in such a way that will show players which are good to pick up and which decks can suit their playstyle!

Consistency

Powerful decks are able to implement a strategy consistently and effectively. Its ability to be effective at all times is a consideration for a deck's placing on this list. Energy efficiency and damage all contribute to a deck's overall consistency.

Flexibility

Facing off against specific deck archetypes would require you to strategize and modify strategies to suit different situations. A deck's ability to pivot to different options is considered for its tier list placing.

Deck List:

Space-Time Smackdown introduces powerful new synergies with cards that naturally go together. Spiritomb spreads damage, while Weavile ex KOs and Cyrus can gust out a damaged Pokemon.

Greninja finds itself a new partner in Gyarados ex, where it provides passive damage, putting opponents in KO range. This archetype also runs the new Druddigon from Mythical Island as a early-game wall.

Palkia ex is a powerful new Misty Pokemon with Manaphy as Energy acceleration. It is able to deal massive damage with Dimensional Storm and do 20 damage to your opponent's Bench, making Cyrus useful for the following turns.

Articuno ex gets valuable new tools from Mythical Island, namely Leaf and Vaporeon. These new additions allow Articuno ex to be more efficient with its Energy from manual attachments and Misty.

Melmetal can reliably hit for 120, which can OHKO most Basic Pokemon and 2HKO exs.

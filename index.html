Tier List Overview

Pokemon TCG Pocket Deck Tier List Overview
Tier Rank Featured Decks

The top tiers of this Tier List (S rank) are Decks that can function with minimal ease for best results. Gyarados ex, Pikachu ex, and Mewtwo ex decks are all quick to set up, relatively powerful and are consistent, which is key to a deck's success.

A+ Tier Decks are also at the top of the metagame but may have to work a little harder to compete against the S Tiers.

A Tier Decks are also powerful and can also function at a high level, but may not be as easy to pilot as the S and A+ Tiers.

B Tier Decks are decent, but would require more skill to achieve success with.

C Tier consists of unexplored Rogue decks, which all have potential but would require a skilled pilot to find success with.

D Tier consists of F2P Friendly decks that are good on their own. These decks have specific niches in the metagame and are outclassed by decks in the higher tiers.

These decks have been arranged in such a way that will show players which are good to pick up and which decks can suit their playstyle!

Consistency

Powerful decks are able to implement a strategy consistently and effectively. Its ability to be effective at all times is a consideration for a deck's placing on this list. Energy efficiency and damage all contribute to a deck's overall consistency.

Flexibility

Facing off against specific deck archetypes would require you to strategize and modify strategies to suit different situations. A deck's ability to pivot to different options is considered for its tier list placing.

Detailed Deck Analysis

S Tier Decks

Weavile ex & Darkrai ex

Space-Time Smackdown introduces powerful new synergies with cards that naturally go together. Spiritomb spreads damage, while Weavile ex KOs and Cyrus can gust out a damaged Pokemon.

Setup Speed Fast
Difficulty Medium
F2P Friendly No

Gyarados ex & Greninja

Greninja finds itself a new partner in Gyarados ex, where it provides passive damage, putting opponents in KO range. This archetype also runs the new Druddigon from Mythical Island as a early-game wall.

Setup Speed Medium
Difficulty Medium
F2P Friendly No

Palkia ex

Palkia ex is a powerful new Misty Pokemon with Manaphy as Energy acceleration. It is able to deal massive damage with Dimensional Storm and do 20 damage to your opponent's Bench, making Cyrus useful for the following turns.

Setup Speed Medium
Difficulty Low
F2P Friendly No

A+ Tier Decks

Vaporeon & Articuno ex

Vaporeon pairs excellently with Articuno ex, providing consistent damage output and energy acceleration. The deck excels at controlling the board with freezing effects while building up powerful attacks.

Setup Speed Medium
Difficulty Medium
F2P Friendly No

Arcanine ex

Arcanine ex is a powerful Fire-type attacker that can quickly set up and deal significant damage. With proper energy acceleration support, it can overwhelm opponents before they have time to establish their strategy.

Setup Speed Fast
Difficulty Low
F2P Friendly No

Dialga ex & Melmetal

Dialga ex pairs with Melmetal to create a formidable Metal-type deck with excellent defensive capabilities. The combination allows for consistent damage output while reducing incoming damage through various effects.

Setup Speed Medium
Difficulty Medium
F2P Friendly No

Pikachu ex & Zebstrika

Pikachu ex teams up with Zebstrika to deliver consistent Electric-type damage. The deck excels at energy acceleration and can quickly power up devastating attacks, making it a threat from the early game.

Setup Speed Fast
Difficulty Low
F2P Friendly No

A Tier Decks

Alakazam & Jynx

Alakazam pairs with Jynx to create a Psychic-type deck focused on control and disruption. The combination allows for consistent damage spread and manipulation of the opponent's board state.

Setup Speed Medium
Difficulty High
F2P Friendly No

Togekiss

Togekiss offers a unique Fairy-type strategy with excellent support capabilities. The deck focuses on building up powerful attacks while providing utility through various abilities and effects.

Setup Speed Slow
Difficulty Medium
F2P Friendly Partial

B Tier Decks

Lapras

Lapras provides a solid Water-type strategy with good defensive capabilities. The deck can stall opponents while building up powerful attacks, making it effective against aggressive strategies.

Setup Speed Medium
Difficulty Medium
F2P Friendly Yes

PorygonZ

PorygonZ offers a unique Normal-type strategy with versatile attack options. The deck can adapt to different matchups and provides consistent damage output throughout the game.

Setup Speed Medium
Difficulty High
F2P Friendly Partial

Rhyperior

Rhyperior provides a powerful Fighting-type strategy with high damage output. The deck excels at taking down big threats but requires careful energy management and setup time.

Setup Speed Slow
Difficulty Medium
F2P Friendly Yes

C Tier Decks

Frosmoth

Frosmoth offers a unique Ice-type strategy focused on energy acceleration for Water-type Pokémon. While powerful when set up, it can struggle against faster decks and requires careful piloting.

Setup Speed Slow
Difficulty High
F2P Friendly Partial

Gengar ex & Golbat

Gengar ex teams up with Golbat to create a Poison-type strategy focused on damage spread and status conditions. The deck can be devastating in the right matchups but struggles against certain archetypes.

Setup Speed Medium
Difficulty High
F2P Friendly No

D Tier Decks

Kabutops

Kabutops offers a budget-friendly Fossil-type strategy that can be effective in certain matchups. While not as powerful as higher-tier decks, it provides a solid entry point for new players with limited resources.

Setup Speed Slow
Difficulty Medium
F2P Friendly Yes
